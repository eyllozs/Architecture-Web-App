OUR PROJECTS
The Broad

In many ways, the architects at Diller Scofidio + Renfro are modern-day magicians. Take, for example, their design of The Broad in Los Angeles.

Elbphilharmonie Hamburg

In its most basic form, Herzog & de Meuron's design for the Elbphilharmonie Hamburg is physical evidence that adaptive reuse can be done to stunning, head-turning effect.

National Museum of Qatar

If the public was hoping to see the billions of dollars of Western art the Qatari's had purchased in the past few decades, then a national museum was a necessity.

Fondation Louis Vuitton

Due to its strict building codes, and architectural pedigree, Paris is among the world's most difficult places to successfully design modern architecture.

The Oculus

Santiago Calatrava has built a reputation for creating structures so dynamic, they appear poised to take flight. And the Spanish-born's design of the Oculus is no exception.

Louvre Abu Dhabi

If the past decade can be viewed as a modern Arab Renaissance for the oil-rich nation, then the Louvre Abu Dhabi is most certainly the centerpiece of this movement.

The Shed

The Shed, which opened in the spring of 2019. The structure consists of an outer shell that sits on a set of wheels connected to a short track.

Apple Park

Apple Park will always be remembered as the final vision of the firm's inimitable founder, Steve Jobs. Apple's latest headquarters will be considered the crowning achievement.

Fass School and Teachers' Residence

Much of architecture is about taking a big vision, localizing it at the community level. Designed by Toshiko Mori, the circular structure was shaped by history of the land.
