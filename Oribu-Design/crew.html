Our Team

With our knowledge and out of the box thinking, we have created a melting pot of brilliant ideas that become reality in immaculate ways.

Our young energy defines us, providing for fresh ideas that defy the beaten path taken by most. We have found that this unusual collaboration that feeds off all aspects of design, produces unique results that cater to the most demanding of clients who we are proud to have in our portfolio.

We’re a diverse practice aligned by a singular passion to change the world with great design.

ORIBU brings together a team for each project individually based on the design task at hand. Our network consists of experienced architects and other skilled professionals in the field of construction. Each project is unique to us – we aim to service each client based on their specific needs and project parameters.

The ORIBU team understands the intricacies of designing places where people live, creating environments that enhance lifestyles and embedding a sense of community in all that we do. We look forward to bringing our commitment to bright vision, bold communities to your project.
